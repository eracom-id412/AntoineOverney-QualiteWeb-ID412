Brands should prioritize sustainability and reduce their environmental impact in order to appeal to environmentally-conscious consumers and contribute to the protection and preservation of the planet.

Statics has chosen to deliver products using web3 and NFT technology and data centers around the world instead of physically transporting items. This decision was made to minimize the company's environmental impact and appeal to consumers who value sustainability. The use of data centers helps to ensure efficiency in the delivery process.
